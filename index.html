Aj Tipsy

Enter

Aj Tipsy who is originally from Zimbabwe, is a young inspired musician who loves performing. He described himself as “Inspirational Song Writer, Musician , Entrepreneur; an individual who has a dream to be a somebody nothing more nothing less. In the past years, he’s been nominated for various Arts Awards (Male Artist Of the Year). He’s among a bunch of U.K based Zimbabwean artists who are rising the flag high within England and nations beyond. Aj Tipsy became well known when he featured on Stunner’s number one hit “Ndimi MaFans. On “Ndimi maFans Tune, Aj Tipsy smashed the chorus. Since then, he’s developed to become a number 1 hit maker on the international music scene. To fully justify his maturity as an artist, Aj recorded and shot a video for his tune “She’s Too Much For Money”. The video was shot and directed by Kazz a.k.a Mr Boomslang
